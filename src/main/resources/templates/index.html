Key Features

User registration and management

Users register via a secure form with email verification. They can view their profile, loan/reservation history and receive personalized recommendations. Librarians and admins are created by the administrator with assigned roles.

Resource search and reservation

Interactive catalog to search books by title, author, or category, with cover/previews upload (local or simulated storage). Simple library location integration (simulation or API).

Loan workflow management

Automated lifecycle: "Reserved", "Borrowed", "In Progress", "Returned", "Closed". Transitions are handled by Spring services with permission checks.

Notifications and communications

Automatic emails via Spring Mail to notify users and librarians. Optional simulated push notifications via WebSockets.

Dashboards and analytics

Role-based dashboards for users, librarians and admins. Analytical reports, charts (Chart.js) and CSV/PDF export.

Advanced search and filtering

Filter by availability, popularity, publication date or category, with pagination and dynamic sorting in Thymeleaf views.
